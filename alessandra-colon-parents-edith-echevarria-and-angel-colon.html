Edith Echevarria And Angel Colon

Publish date: 2024-07-08

Now that you have loved to see Dr. Alessandra Colón encountering a variety of patients, from genetic defects to run-of-the-mill back problems, on TLC’s Crack Addicts, here’s a tell-all about who her parents are.

Who Are TLC’s Crack Addicts Star Alessandra Colon’s Parents?

TLC’s Crack Addicts star Alessandra Colon has always been fond of her family, including her parents. Even when she was 14 and found out that she had Stage 4 cancer she was mostly just worried about her family. At that point, her family had moved back to the United States after having lived in Saudi Arabia and other locales.

Keep reading to learn more about her parents.

Meet Edith Echevarria, Alessandra Colon’s Mother

One can tell Alessandra Colon is very fond of her mother Edith Echevarria. “To the mother who paved the way to make all my dreams come true”, she wrote this recently next to a lovely picture of Edith.

And before that, on her “beautiful mama’s” birthday in 2021, she took to her social media again to tell her just how closest she feels to her. “In fact, I’ve loved getting to know you even more deeply than I ever thought I could. The stories you have told me about your life only make me feel that much closer to you —- you could write a book”, Alessandra gushed.

Because of her mother, Alexandra has never thought of getting older as a terrible thing. Through her mother, she has instead seen maturity, wisdom, and experience inevitably coming with age only.

  • Edith Echevarria Age

On 19 November 2021, Edith Echevarria’s daughter let everyone know that her mother just turned 70 writing “HERES TO BIG 70… you have never looked more radiant!”. So, in 2022, Edith reached the age of 71.

  • Edith Echevarria Job

Per LinkedIn, Edith Echevarria has had a career as a medical technician at Baptist Health Bethesda Hospital in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida, United States. And before that, she studied a medical technician course at Universidad de PR, Mayaguez it seems.

  • Is Edith Echevarria On Instagram?

Yes. Edith Echevarria can be found on Instagram. But, as of 4 June 2023, the account @eechevarria38 included no posts and only a few followers.

Luckily, on Facebook, Edith did show now and then glimpses from her life.

Meet Angel Colon, Alessandra Colon’s Father

Alessandra Colon also has an exceptional relationship with her father. In June 2021, Alessandra showed her people on social media an old-time memory of Angel walking her down the aisle for her “15th”. She was maybe referring to her 15th birthday. Below this post, Angel was seen commenting “I remember that day as if yesterday…and the one from during your Chemo treatment…I saved those two…love Papa…”.

Other times, we have also seen Angel calling his daughter “My beautiful LLS woman of the year in Florida” or appreciating her healthy salads.

  • Angel Colon Age

Angel D Colon was born in March 1952. So, he reached the age of 71 in 2023.

  • Angel Colon Job

Based on what Angel Colon writes on Facebook, he is a sole proprietor of A.E. Innovations Inc- O & G Topside Facility Design. Also, he mentioned working at Westlake Petrochemical Complex Lake Charles Louisiana since 20 May 2023. Before all this, Angel studied BSChE Chemical Engineering at the University of Puerto Rico at Mayagüez.

  • Is Angel Colon On Instagram?

Angel Colon could not be found on Instagram as of 2023. He did seem to be enjoying being on Facebook though.

Related FAQs

  • Are Alessandra Colon’s Parents Still Married?

Alessandra Colon’s parents were no longer married as of 2023. On Facebook, her father mentioned his relationship status as ‘single’. Edith, however, got married in 2021.

On November 1st of that year, she uploaded a picture from her wedding day, and when below it a friend of his commented: “Didn’t know you were getting married!!”, she replied with a “It was a surprise to many, including my family.”

  • How Many Children Do Alessandra Colon’s Parents Have?

Alessandra Colon is not the only child of her parents. There are more.

One of them is Daniel Colón. According to his Facebook, Daniel is a storyboard artist at Boards In Motion Inc, he studied Art History at Hunter College and Painting at Maryland Institute College of Art. Also, he went to Cranbrook Kingswood and Jubail International School. At the time of this writing, he had been residing in Bronx, New York.

“I’m so proud to see my sister, Alessandra, featured by LLS on #givingtuesday and to be a supporter of such an impactful organization. See her story below and please donate if you can–you really can make a difference!”, this is what Daniel wrote in support of his sister on a social media post not long ago.

Other than all of them, Alexandra, in her life, also has her husband David R Lauck of Palm Beach Gardens, Florida. He reached the age of 43 in May 2023. On his IG @lauck_down, he writes “Traveling is my drug💉💊, I need more of it.”

  • Where Do Alessandra Colon’s Parents Reside?

As of 2023, Alessandra’s mother called Palm Beach Gardens, Florida his home. On the other hand, Angel had been residing in Lake Charles, Louisiana. Originally, he hails from Houston, Texas.
