Sam Mark from Netflix's The Ultimatum: Queer Love appears in the show with her partner, Aussie Chau. While we're unaware of the 31-year-old's job, you can find her on Instagram at (@aussie_has_fomo).

The Ultimatum: Queer Love on Netflix, hosted by JoAnna Garcia Swisher, puts queer relationships to the test by giving one individual an ultimatum: propose or end the relationship. Each participant temporarily breaks up with their significant other, goes on dates, and selects a new partner from the other participating couples to begin a trial marriage in order to gain a fresh perspective on their relationships and what they want out of life as well as a partner.

Sam Mark appears on the show alongside her partner, Aussie Chau. The couple met as members of an online social club during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, and they've been together ever since. But, while Sam is eager to marry and start a family, Aussie isn't convinced marriage is in the cards.

Those who have seen the first four episodes know that Aussie ends up with Mildred Woody, while Sam Mark and Tiff Der are paired up for their trial marriage. Will Sam and Aussie ever find their way back to each other? Find out more about Sam and what to anticipate from the show in the article below!
