EveryBodyKnowsLo: Rapper killed in nightclub shooting at Miami Beach, 2 women injured

Publish date: 2024-08-21

MIAMI BEACH, FLORIDA: Rapper Lowell Grissom, better known as EveryBodyKnowsLO, has reportedly been killed in a shooting that also injured two women at a Miami Beach nightclub on Sunday, May 7. The incident took place before 4 am when the Miami Beach Police Department received a 911 call regarding shots fired at the address of the GALA nightclub in the city’s South Beach area.

Upon arrival, the responding officers found three gunshot victims - one male and two females, who were transported to Ryder Trauma Centre. Despite doctors’ relentless efforts, Grissom died in hospital due to his injuries. However, the authorities noted that the two injured women are expected to recover soon. Police have launched an investigation but the motive behind the shooting is as yet unknown. A man, who was at his job near the nightclub when bullets were fired, recalled, “The club’s security ran out to the street. The women ran out of the club crying and were all shaken up.”

Grissom, better known as EveryBodyKnowsLO, was a rapper and talent scout for Chris Brown Entertainment, . On Sunday, Chris Brown paid him a moving tribute with a social media post that read, “Love you, bro, Rest up my Brother.” He also had a well-known social media presence, with more than 56.1k followers.

Following the shooting, the GALA nightclub released a statement on social media. “We are shocked and deeply saddened by the isolated and targeted incident that occurred last night at our upscale establishment. Our condolences are with the family and friends of the victim during this time. We are fully committed to providing assistance and cooperating with the Miami Beach PD as they conduct their ongoing investigation,” the statement read. Police have not provided any further details about the incidents leading up to the shooting, but they have urged anybody with information to call Miami-Dade Crime Stoppers at 305-471-TIPS.
