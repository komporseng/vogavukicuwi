Andrew Santino siblings: Ali Mac

Publish date: 2024-09-01

Andrew Santino is an American actor and stand-up comedian. He also works as a podcast co-host.

He was born in Chicago, Illinois, on October 16, 1983. Since making his breakthrough he has become quite a popular figure in stand-up comedy.

He is also renowned for his performances as an actor, after appearances in The Disaster Artist, Sin City Saints, Mixology, and Dave.  Andrew was also featured in the Showtime special Home Field Advantage.

In 2020, he and Bobby Lee started co-hosting the Bad Friends podcast. However, the biggest role he has landed in his career is undoubtedly his portrayal of Allen Geller in Me Time, where he starred alongside the likes of Kevin Hart and Mark Wahlberg.

Andrew Santino siblings: Ali Mac

Just like Andrew, Ali Mac was brought up in River North in the state of Chicago. He was raised in a single-mother household and his mother worked several jobs to provide for both Ali and his brother.
