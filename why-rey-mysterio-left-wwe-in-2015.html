Why Rey Mysterio Left WWE in 2015

Publish date: 2024-07-14

Rey Mysterio, a renowned figure in the wrestling industry, left WWE in 2015, sparking curiosity among fans and industry insiders.

In this detailed article, we will delve into the reasons behind Rey Mysterio's departure from the WWE during that year. We will analyze the factors that led to this decision, shedding light on the physical, mental, and familial aspects that influenced Mysterio's exit from the prominent wrestling promotion.

Career Legacy and Success in WWE

Rey Mysterio, an iconic luchador, made a name for himself in WWE after an illustrious career in WCW's cruiserweight division. Upon joining WWE in 2002, Mr. 619 achieved remarkable success, winning titles such as the World Heavyweight Championship, WWE Championship, Intercontinental Title, Cruiserweight Title, and multiple Tag Team Championships. His high-flying wrestling style and memorable feuds solidified his status as an international wrestling superstar.

Burnout and Family Reconnection

In an interview with The National, the WWE legend candidly revealed the primary reasons behind his departure from WWE in 2015.

Expressing his physical and mental exhaustion, he emphasized the need to reconnect with his family, citing these as pivotal factors in his decision to leave the company.

The rigorous schedule and demanding nature of professional wrestling had taken a toll on Mysterio, prompting him to prioritize his familial relationships and well-being.

WWE's Rigorous Schedule

The demanding nature of WWE's schedule is well-documented, often leading to burnout among wrestlers. Mysterio's departure underscores the challenges faced by performers who are constantly on the road, with limited time for personal and family commitments.

His departure sheds light on the toll that WWE's intense schedule can take on wrestlers, emphasizing the need for a balance between professional and personal life.

Renewed Commitment to Family and WWE

Despite his departure in 2015, Mysterio expressed his unwavering desire to return to WWE and eventually retire in the company.

His commitment to WWE and the desire to conclude his wrestling career within the organization remained steadfast. Mysterio's return to WWE after an 11-month hiatus, followed by his subsequent re-signing with the company in 2018, underscores his enduring connection and loyalty to WWE.

Contractual Deadlock and Departure

The circumstances surrounding Mysterio's departure from WWE were somewhat complex, with reports of a contractual deadlock between the two parties. Mysterio's intention to leave the promotion was complicated by an extension of his contract due to an injury in 2014, leading to negotiations that ultimately did not yield an agreement.

This impasse, alongside reported communication breakdowns, contributed to the uncertainty surrounding Mysterio's contractual status with WWE.

Legacy and Future in WWE

Mysterio's legacy in WWE and his enduring impact on the wrestling industry are undeniable. His departure in 2015, marked by physical and mental fatigue, underscored the human side of professional wrestling and the inherent challenges faced by performers.

As Mysterio continues his journey in WWE, he remains committed to concluding his illustrious career within the company, emphasizing the profound connection he and his family share with WWE.
