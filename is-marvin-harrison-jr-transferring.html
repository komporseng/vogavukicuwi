Is Marvin Harrison Jr transferring?

Publish date: 2024-05-28

In college football, the transfer portal has ushered in a new era when athletes can switch clubs at any time, even when it first seems unlikely. Therefore, it doesn’t take much for people to accept an internet rumour claiming wide receiver Marvin Harrison Jr. is moving from Ohio State to USC.

Is Marvin Harrison Jr transferring?

Well, it seems the rumour is untrue. Harrison reportedly expressed shock when Jeremy Birmingham, an Ohio State source at Rivals.com, personally questioned him about the claim.

Birmingham said on his show, “I asked him when he’s packing his stuff to transfer to USC.” And he said, “Where the—do they come up with this stuff?” I quote.

Therefore, Birmingham assured Buckeyes fans that Harrison is not going anywhere.

“If you’re worried that Marvin Harrison Jr. is transferring to USC, please don’t,” he said.
