Photos Of The 90210 Actress Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-08-03

Tori Spelling, born May 16, 1973, is an actress, author, and reality star. Tori was a child of Hollywood, as the daughter of TV creating giant Aaron Spelling. Her first big part was in ‘90210’, where she played Donna Martin. She would reprise the role for the 2019 spin-off ‘BH90210’. Tori’s other credits include ‘A Friend to Die For’ (1994), both versions of ‘Mother, May I Sleep with Danger?’ (1996 & 2016) and ‘The Last Sharknado: It’s About Time (2018)’. Big screen credits include ‘Scary Movie 2’ (2001), ‘Cthulhu’ (2007), ‘Kiss the Bride’ (2007) and ‘Izzie’s Way Home’. In addition to her acting, Tori wrote the New York Times Bestselling memoir ‘Stori Telling’. She is also a mother of 5 with estranged husband Dean McDermott.
