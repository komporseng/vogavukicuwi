NFL Star Jonathan Owens: A Closer Look at the Husband of Simone Biles

Publish date: 2024-05-04

When you are a fan of sports, you become a bit addicted to the lives of athletes, particularly when they intertwine with pop culture.

On that note, we will be diving deep into the life of Jonathan Owens, the wonderful husband of the legendary gymnast, and Olympian, Simone Biles.

Utilizing his background, his professional football journey, and his heartwarming relationship with Simone, we will dive into the exciting story of this NFL player.


The Man Behind the Name

Jonathan Owens is a Safety for the Chicago Bears, born on Oct. 21, 1995 in St. Louis, Mo.

The story of how he made his way into the professional football world is one of grit and grind. Owens has been an all-out, all-the-time player from his high school football games to his current gig in the NFL.


A Football Odyssey

Owens began his NFL career with the Arizona Cardinals in 2018 and spent 2019 on the Houston Texans practice squad.

But through his dedication and talent lead to him signing with the Texans the next year.

After that, in a widely-watched move, he then became a free agent and agreed to terms in May 2023 with the Green Bay Packers, continuing his exciting football story.

There have been ups and downs along the way, but Owens can overcome anything, just ask the NFL.


A Love Story

A chance meeting with four-time Olympic gold medalist Simone Biles changed the course of Jonathan Owens' life.

Their story that bloomed, juggling their high-pressure work life, is intriguing. The two crossed paths in March of 2020 and the rest has been a sweet ride since then.

The pair share very strong values and have an excellent understanding, as evidenced by their relationship.

A seamless fit, best explained by two people sharing a common sense of humor, a commitment to unwavering and ebullient support for the other; a real love and appreciation for the other's separate athletic endeavors.

Their love and the dedication they have shown to one another have struck a chord with many on an international scale.

A Final Word

The story of Jonathan Owens and Simone Biles is the perfect example of all of that, amid sports and love.

The tale is a heartwarming journey from two strangers meeting by chance to their marriage in 2023. Jonathan was able to forge a successful path to a career in the NFL, and Simone is in the midst of an incredible journey in the Olympics.

They accomplished great things, and their collective story serves as a powerful testament to the lasting power of love, encouragement, and the relentless pursuit of their aspirations.
