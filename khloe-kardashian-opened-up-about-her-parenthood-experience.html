Khloe Kardashian Opened Up About Her Parenthood Experience

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Khloe Kardashian, an American socialite, has claimed that she is thrilled to be the mother of two children. In an interview with Elle Magazine on August 30, 2022, the 38-year-old actress raved about her parenthood experience with daughter True and newborn baby, whom she shares with ex-partner Tristan Thompson. She stated:

“I know it’s cliche, but I like everything, including the difficult bits.” My children push me as a person, and shaping tiny people into really great large people is a privilege and a blessing.”

Khloe Kardashian gave birth to her second child via surrogacy earlier this month. US Weekly was the first to report that Khloe Kardashian and Thompson had a baby boy early this month. According to one of the publication’s sources:

“Both the baby and the mother are in good health. Khloé is overjoyed to grow her family.”

Khloe also addressed parenting in today’s culture in her ELLE interview, stating:

“We have to take those duties seriously, particularly in this day and age, with how much access children have and how much information they are exposed to at such a young age.” It’s quite frightening, but I take my work very seriously. It’s fantastic.”

Khloe Kardashian is speaking up for the first time about her second kid with Thompson.

Khloe Kardashian and Tristan Thompson had a difficult relationship.

Tristan Thompson and Khloe Kardashian started dating in 2016. In 2018, the couple had their first child, a girl called True. They dated intermittently until 2021. The former couple used surrogacy to have their second child in November 2021. However, Tristan was caught cheating on the Keeping Up with the Kardashians star in March 2021, while they were still dating.

The NBA star had a baby, Theo, with Maralee Nichols, with whom he cheated on Khloe Kardashian in December 2021. Thompson made a statement stating he is the father after a paternity test. He also apologized for putting Khloe through this experience. Khloe and Tristan announced in July 2022 that they were expecting their second child together through surrogacy.

True is ecstatic to have a newborn brother.

According to a source who recently talked with Hollywood Life, True Thompson is happy to have a newborn brother and is often at his side. According to the source:

“Khloe thinks it’s really lovely to watch True take up the big sister position on this, and she’s glad to see that True doesn’t have any jealous problems, which she was concerned about.”

According to another source, True has been quite careful with her new baby brother.

According to the source:

“With her newborn brother, Khloe has seen another side of True, and it’s the greatest thing ever.” True recognizes the distinction since he is her brother, and she is patient and gentle with him. True approaches him with utmost tenderness so she does not injure him.”

Despite the fact that the couple has another kid together, a source informed Us Weekly Magazine that they are no longer together, adding that they “have not communicated outside of co-parenting responsibilities” since December 2021.
