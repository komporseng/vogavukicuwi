Pokémon world locations

A Mirage Mountain (Japanese: マボロシ山 Phantom Mountain) is a location in Hoenn located on an island in Pokémon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire. It is only accessible by soaring on LatiasAS or LatiosOR. A Mirage Mountain can appear daily, or it may not appear, being replaced with a Mirage Island, Mirage Forest, or Mirage Cave. More Mirage spots can appear via StreetPass.

If a Mirage Mountain is received via StreetPass, it will not have a hidden item.

In the Pokémon Omega Ruby and Pokémon Alpha Sapphire Special Demo Version, two of the Mirage Mountains can also be visited during play.

Mirage Mountains

Each Mirage Mountain contains one item that can only be obtained once, as well as one of three possible items (Green Shard, Yellow Shard, or Deep Sea Scale) that will be present whenever it appears as the daily Mirage spot; the unique item is listed below. Additionally, some Mirage Mountains include breakable rocks, which the player can destroy with Rock Smash to encounter wild Pokémon or obtain fossils.
