Who is Jamie Lee Curtis?

Jamie Lee Curtis is the legendary American actress who played Laurie in the Halloween sequels. Her career had a big start by featuring as Lt. Barbara Duran on the ABC sitcom Operation Petticoat (1977-78).

Likewise, she has won two Golden Globe Awards, a British Academy Film Award, and is a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. Jamie Curtis is also a frequent blogger for The Huffington Post.

Not only an actress but she has written numerous children’s books Today I Feel Silly, and Other Moods That Make My Day, and ultimately, included in The New York Times‘s best-seller list.
